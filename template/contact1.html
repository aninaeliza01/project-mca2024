What are the requirements to become a delivery partner?

The requirements to become a delivery partner may vary depending on the delivery service provider. However, common requirements may include having a valid driver's license, a reliable vehicle or mode of transportation, good communication skills, and the ability to navigate efficiently.

Is prior delivery experience required?

Prior delivery experience may be preferred by some delivery service providers, but it is not always required. Many providers offer training programs for new delivery partners to help them learn the necessary skills and procedures.
